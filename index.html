Anxiety


What is Anxiety?


Anxiety is a common mental health condition characterized by excessive worry, fear, and nervousness. It can manifest in various forms, including generalized anxiety disorder, panic disorder, and social anxiety disorder.

Common symptoms of anxiety

Anxiety can affect the body and mind in many ways. These symptoms often appear together and can vary in intensity from day to day.

Learn more

Physical tension

Muscle tightness, restlessness, a racing heart, or feeling on edge.

Overthinking

Persistent worry, difficulty concentrating, or racing thoughts.

Heightened alertness

Feeling easily startled, overwhelmed, or constantly “on guard.”

Avoidance

Steering clear of situations that trigger worry or discomfort.
